What Do We Do Exactly

Here at IEC we strive to provide the best image editing in the business. We have all had moment where we take pictures of moments that we want to remember, but when we see those pictures years later, we're not sure exactly what we're looking at, or we look a lot worse than we remembered. There are so many small things that can ruin a photo that you wouldn't even think of in the moment. One of our most popular request is to whiten teeth in people's smiles, and to cut stray people out of the background. Another popular request is to remove the glare on certain objects.

What Are Some Changes You May Want?

  1. As stated above, One of our most popular request is to whiten the clients teeth to make their smile look better
  2. Also stated above our second most requested action is to cut unwanted people/objects/animals/etc. From the background.
  3. Sometimes people just want their picture to be a little less grey and a lot more colorful, so we can breathe life into any image you want.
